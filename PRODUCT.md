# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Daily shipment operators and business owners who need to review one or more monthly shipment Excel workbooks without manually filtering rows.

## Product Purpose

The product is a local drag-and-drop shipment dashboard. A user drops one or more Excel workbooks into the app, and it extracts today's rows from the `发货明细` sheet, then summarizes customers, product models, quantities, and amounts in a clear visual dashboard.

Success means the user can understand today's shipment customers, models, quantities, and amount distribution within a few seconds, then export the detail if needed.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, practical.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page styling, oversized decorative sections, generic AI gradients, dark technical dashboards, and interfaces that hide the table behind visual decoration.

## Design Principles

- Make the daily action obvious: drop files first, read results second.
- Put total numbers above exploration details.
- Use charts to clarify priority, not to decorate.
- Keep the exact table close to the chart so numbers remain auditable.
- Prefer familiar desktop-business patterns over novelty.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable Chinese UI text, strong contrast, keyboard-accessible file selection, visible focus states, and motion that is subtle enough not to interrupt daily work.
